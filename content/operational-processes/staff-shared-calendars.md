# Staff Shared Calendars

## Umuzi Annual Calendar

The Umuzi Annual Calendar will serve as a central hub where you can access important dates and details related to company activities throughout the year.

### What's Included:

- **All-hands Meetings:** Stay aware of our regular company-wide meetings where we share updates, celebrate achievements, and align on our goals.
- **Umuzi Holidays:** Mahala days off. There is no need to apply for annual leave. You're welcome!
- **Staff Leave:** SageHR is synced with the Umuzi Annual Calendar! You are able to see who's on leave as well as upcoming birthdays directly in the shared calendar.
- **Important Recurring Meetings:** Regular team check-ins, departmental syncs, and other key meetings will be available to help you stay on top of things.
- **Training and Development Sessions:** Mark your calendars for upcoming internal training, workshops, and learning opportunities.
- **In-person Events:** Remote work is challenging. It's important to monitor in-person work weeks and maintain connections.
