# Key Performance Agreements (KPA)

## Why Are Individual KPAs Important?

### Aligns Individual Efforts with Umuzi Objectives & Goals

- Ensures that each team member's work contributes to Umuzi's mission and objectives.
- **Example:** If Umuzi's goal is to build thought leadership, a KPA like "Publish one Umuzi Pulse per year" ensures alignment.

### Provides Clear Expectations & Accountability

- Employees know exactly what's expected of them.
- Helps avoid ambiguity around performance measurement.

### Enables Performance Tracking & Growth

- KPAs provide measurable data on progress.
- Regular tracking helps identify strengths and areas for improvement.

### Motivates & Engages Employees

- Clear goals can drive motivation by giving employees a sense of purpose.
- Achievable KPAs create a sense of accomplishment and recognition.

### Facilitates Constructive Feedback & Development

- Managers can use KPA data to provide specific, actionable feedback.
- Helps employees focus on professional development and career growth.

### Supports Data-Driven Decision Making

- Leadership can assess performance trends and optimise resource allocation.
- Helps identify top performers and those who may need additional support.

---

## Who Is Responsible for Setting KPAs?

Managers & employees co-create KPAs to ensure buy-in and motivation. Regular check-ins and feedback loops help refine and adjust KPAs.

### Leadership Role

- Provides guidelines on KPA structures and performance evaluation.
- Ensures KPAs align with Umuzi's strategy and are fair across teams.
- Facilitates performance reviews and adjustments if needed.

### Manager's Responsibility

- Aligns individual KPAs with Umuzi's goals and team objectives.
- Ensures KPAs are realistic, measurable, and achievable.
- Provides clarity on expectations and tracks progress.

### Employee's Responsibility

- Contributes to defining their own KPAs based on role, strengths, and career goals.
- Takes ownership of progress and performance.
- Regularly updates manager on progress and seeks support if needed.
