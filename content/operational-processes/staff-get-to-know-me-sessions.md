# Staff: Get to Know Me Sessions

## What are "Get to Know Me" Sessions?

Working remotely means we don't always get to share those casual coffee chats or spontaneous office moments that help us truly get to know each other. These sessions create space for us to connect beyond our screens, learn about each other's journeys, and build a stronger, more supportive team.

It's amazing what we discover when we take a moment to listen, whether it's a hidden talent, an inspiring story, or just a hilarious fun fact. Every teammate has a story worth sharing, and this is our way of celebrating that.

Once a month, a mystery guest is chosen, and the team keeps throwing guesses to the Team WhatsApp group leading up to the big reveal on Friday. It's a chance to learn about our teammates in a way that goes beyond job titles and tasks.
