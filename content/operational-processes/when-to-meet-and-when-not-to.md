# Async vs. Synchronous: When to Meet and When Not To

## What's the Difference?

- **Synchronous Communication** happens in real-time, like meetings, calls, and live chats/huddles.
- **Asynchronous Communication** happens on your own time, like emails, recorded videos, and shared docs.

Both have their place, but knowing when to use each can save time, increase productivity, and reduce meeting fatigue.

## Making the Right Call: Async or Sync?

Before scheduling a meeting, ask yourself:

1. Can this be done in an email, Slack thread, or shared doc instead?
2. Do we need immediate back-and-forth conversation?
3. Will async allow for better thoughtfulness and participation?
4. Are all participants available at the same time, or will time zones be a challenge?
