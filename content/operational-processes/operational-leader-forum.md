# Operational Leadership Forum

## Purpose

The Operational Leadership Forum is a senior biweekly meeting focused on execution, unblocking, and cross-functional coordination. Its primary goal is to support improved operational functioning and collaboration across Umuzi's senior team. This is where action gets aligned and momentum builds.

It replaces Circles and SA Ops in the calendar, among other meetings. It will support the organisational restructure, promote information sharing and enable more efficiency through opportunity spotting. It is also an opportunity for up-and-coming leaders to engage with exco more cross-functionally to support their personal development.

## Meeting Culture

This forum is a place where our culture comes alive. Our cameras are on. We are engaged. We expect everyone to show up as leaders. **No passive observers. No spectators.**

We also:

- Keep our learners at the centre
- Cultivate joy
- Practice accountability
- Build trust
- Foster belief
- Accelerate learning
- Inspire action

## Format

Each function presents for **5 minutes**, covering:

- Projects on the go
- Up-and-coming things to be aware of
- Cross-functional blockers
